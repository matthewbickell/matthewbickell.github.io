My Experiences

Company

Working for GameStop was an overall positive experience. I worked with a friendly and supportive team who shared similar interests in video games and pop culture. Management was strict only when it came to confidential information such as upcoming releases and sales events.

Customers

Customer interactions were a mix of personalities. Most customers were kind, loyal, and enjoyed discussing games and recommendations. Occasionally, upset customers appeared, but problems could usually be resolved quickly by staying calm and professional.

Takeaway

Overall, GameStop was an excellent early job that helped me build strong customer service skills. If you enjoy video games and pop culture, it is a great environment to make friends and gain retail experience while sharing your interests with others.
